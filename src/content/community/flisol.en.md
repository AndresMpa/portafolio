# FLISoL

## Overview

FLISoL (Festival Latinoamericano de Instalación de Software Libre) is the largest Free and Open Source Software event in Latin America and one of the most important community-driven technology initiatives in the region.

As the leader and organizer of FLISoL Pereira, I have coordinated volunteers, speakers, universities, sponsors and local communities to create events focused on Linux, Free Software, Open Source technologies and technical education.

My involvement extends beyond event logistics and includes community building, public speaking, technical mentoring and the promotion of open technologies throughout Colombia.

---

## Community Leadership

Leading FLISoL Pereira has required coordinating multidisciplinary teams composed of:

- Software engineers
- Students
- Community leaders
- Open source contributors
- Academic institutions
- Technology enthusiasts

Together we have organized conferences, workshops, installfests, community networking activities and technical discussions focused on software freedom and knowledge sharing.

The objective has always been to create accessible spaces where people can discover, learn and contribute to open technologies.

## Educational Impact

One of the most rewarding aspects of FLISoL has been its educational impact.

The events have provided opportunities for students, developers and professionals to learn about:

- Software Freedom
- Programming
- Artificial Intelligence
- Cloud Computing
- Developer Tooling
- Cybersecurity
- Emerging Technologies

By connecting experienced professionals with newcomers, FLISoL helps strengthen local technology communities and encourages participation in global open source ecosystems.
