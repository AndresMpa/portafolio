# Arch Linux

## Overview

Arch Linux has been my primary operating system for many years and has played a central role in both my professional work and my contributions to the open source ecosystem.

Although I am not part of the core Arch Linux development team, I actively contribute to the community by sharing technical knowledge, helping users troubleshoot complex Linux issues, publishing documentation, maintaining open source tooling and participating in technical events focused on Linux and Free Software.

My work aligns with the Arch philosophy of simplicity, transparency and continuous learning, encouraging developers to understand how their systems work instead of relying solely on abstractions. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Open Source Philosophy

One of the reasons I have remained active in the Arch Linux community is its educational approach.

The project encourages users to understand system internals, configuration management and software installation processes instead of hiding complexity behind graphical tools. This philosophy has significantly influenced my own engineering practices and the way I design software, infrastructure and developer tools. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Many of my public projects—including Linux utilities, shell automation, Neovim configurations and AI tooling—have been developed with this same mindset of transparency, documentation and long-term maintainability.
