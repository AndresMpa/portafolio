# Café FLISoL - Can AI Take My Job?

## Overview

This talk was delivered as part of Café FLISoL, a community-driven technology discussion space broadcast through Ubuntu Colombia channels.

The live session focused on one of the most frequently asked questions in the software industry during 2024:

> Can artificial intelligence replace software developers?

Throughout the discussion, I explored the real impact of AI on software engineering, the misconceptions surrounding automation, and the opportunities available to professionals willing to adapt to an AI-driven future.

The event attracted approximately 100 live attendees who actively participated through questions and discussions about the future of software development.

---

## Context

The rapid adoption of generative AI technologies created significant uncertainty across the technology sector during 2024.

Many developers were concerned about:

- Automated code generation.
- AI-powered development assistants.
- Changes in software engineering workflows.
- Job displacement.
- The future role of software engineers.

This talk aimed to provide a practical and technically grounded perspective based on industry trends and professional experience.

---

## Main Topics

Key topics covered included:

- The current state of artificial intelligence.
- What AI can realistically automate.
- Limitations of large language models.
- The future evolution of software engineering roles.
- Critical thinking in the age of AI.
- Open Source and artificial intelligence.
- Emerging career opportunities.
- Skills required for the next decade.

The session emphasized that AI should be viewed as a productivity amplifier rather than simply a replacement technology.

## Key Takeaways

Some of the main conclusions shared during the session included:

- AI is transforming work more than replacing it.
- Human skills remain essential.
- Understanding systems is more valuable than memorizing tools.
- Continuous learning creates competitive advantages.
- Open Source will remain a key driver of innovation.
- Developers who learn to collaborate with AI will gain significant professional opportunities.
