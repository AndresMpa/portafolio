# FLISoL UTP 2024: 20 Years Promoting Digital Freedom

## Overview

In 2024 I was interviewed by **Universidad Tecnológica de Pereira** about **FLISoL Pereira 2024**, where I served as the lead organizer.

The interview highlighted the work behind organizing one of the largest Free Software events in Colombia's Coffee Region and showcased how the local Open Source community has continued to grow through collaboration, education and knowledge sharing.

The event welcomed approximately **500 attendees** and featured **more than twenty technical talks**, workshops and community sessions focused on Linux, Free Software and Open Source technologies.

---

## Background

FLISoL (Festival Latinoamericano de Instalación de Software Libre) is the largest Free Software community event in Latin America, celebrated simultaneously across dozens of cities every year.

The 2024 Pereira edition also commemorated the festival's twentieth anniversary, bringing together students, software professionals, technology communities and organizations interested in Free Software and digital freedom.

The interview documented both the event itself and the collaborative work required to organize it.

## Organizing FLISoL Pereira

As the event lead, I contributed to several areas, including:

- Technical program coordination.
- Speaker management.
- Event logistics.
- Volunteer coordination.
- Community outreach.
- Public promotion of Free Software.

The result was an event attended by roughly five hundred participants with more than twenty technical presentations throughout the day.

---

## Impact

FLISoL Pereira 2024 strengthened the regional technology ecosystem by creating an open space where developers, students and technology enthusiasts could exchange knowledge, discover Open Source projects and build professional connections.

The interview also highlighted how community-driven initiatives can produce high-impact technology events through volunteer collaboration.
