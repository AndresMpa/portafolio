# FLISoL Pereira 2023

## Overview

FLISoL Pereira 2023 marked the return of the Latin American Free Software Installation Festival to Pereira after several years without an official local edition.

As one of the event leaders, I coordinated a team of approximately **25 organizers** to deliver a two-day festival that welcomed **more than 1,000 attendees** through technical talks, workshops, Linux installation sessions and community activities hosted at the Technological University of Pereira.

Participants traveled from multiple regions of Colombia and even from other countries, making this the largest technology event I have led to date. The event also officially reintroduced Pereira into the Latin American FLISoL network. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

---

## Background

FLISoL is the largest Free Software outreach event in Latin America, taking place simultaneously across dozens of cities.

The Pereira edition aimed to rebuild the local Free Software community while connecting universities, developers and technology enthusiasts interested in Linux, Open Source and collaborative software development. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

---

## My Role

As one of the event leaders, I coordinated:

- Overall event planning.
- A team of approximately 25 organizers.
- The academic conference program.
- Workshops and Linux installation sessions.
- Community outreach.
- Volunteer coordination.
- Collaboration with the Technological University of Pereira.

The objective was to reestablish Pereira as an active contributor to the Latin American Free Software community.
