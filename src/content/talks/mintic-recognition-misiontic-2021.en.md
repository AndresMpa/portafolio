# Recognition for Excellence from Colombia's Ministry of ICT at Misión TIC 2021

## Overview

In 2021, I received a public recognition from Colombia's Ministry of Information and Communications Technologies (MinTic) during the closing ceremony of the national Misión TIC 2021 program.

The recognition was awarded as a success story of the initiative after achieving the highest possible academic performance during the program and successfully transitioning into the technology industry as an Artificial Intelligence Engineer during the same year.

The recognition was delivered publicly by the acting Vice Minister of ICT during the official ceremony held at Universidad Tecnológica de Pereira, in front of an audience of approximately 500 attendees.

---

## About Misión TIC

Misión TIC was one of the largest technology education initiatives promoted by the Colombian government.

Its objective was to accelerate the training of software developers and technology professionals capable of supporting the country's digital transformation.

The program trained thousands of students nationwide in software development, engineering fundamentals and digital skills.

---

## Why This Recognition Was Awarded

The recognition highlighted two major achievements:

- Outstanding academic performance throughout the program.
- Successful entry into the technology industry as an Artificial Intelligence Engineer immediately after graduation.

The award demonstrated not only technical achievement but also the practical impact of technology education programs in creating professional opportunities.
